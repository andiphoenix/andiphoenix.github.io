About

My name is Andi Phoenix.

Born in Kyrgyzstan and raised in a small city called Detmold, NRW.
I used to be a calm and shy boy in my teenage times,
only in video games I've felt comfortable enough to be me and let out
all the thoughts that defined me.
This changed dramaticaly when I moved to Cologne to study Event-Management.
My interest in Games vanished instantly when I found out that I am actually into guys and since then my self-esteem developed in immense speed.
I've found the love of my life quickly and so I became the person I am today.
I've been playing music instruments since I was eight years old
and I've been playing in bands for 10 years now.
I sing in my band "Desasterkids" for six years now and we are on tour regularly.

Some weeks ago I decided to learn web programming because I was unhappy with
the way my career was going and I've felt like I needed to learn something
completly new and start a new chapter in my life.
This is my very first website. The beginning of a new path. A new life.
My legacy.
